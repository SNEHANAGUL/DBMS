About Us

A Leading Medical Service Provider

ResQRoutes ambulance services are one of the pioneers in emergency medical care and services at all instances. Since the inception, we are providing reliable critical care and emergency services. We have recorded high success rates of moving critical patients to the hospital on time.

An organisation with a cause. That’s how we define ourselves. Our goal is to save lives by helping people get an Ambulance in the shortest possible time at the lowest cost that they’re able to negotiate and are comfortable.

Just like how easy it is to get a cab or order food, so should be getting an Ambulance when in need of any kind of medical transport.

ResQRoutes's vision is to assist in saving human lives by providing a leading network of fully equipped Advanced and Basic Life Support Ambulances across the developing world.

Our vision is reflected in our commitment to meet International standards of quality in Emergency Medical Services and be accessible to everyone regardless of their income bracket.

Our core values are being ethical, being transparent and fostering teamwork. Ziqitza's focus has always been on delivering top quality services that involve saving lives, minimising anguish and aiding in improving the patient’s outcome.

For us every life matters and every second counts in saving a life. We believe that countless lives can be saved and numerous casualties minimized, with adequate and quick medical transportation facilities
